Hiking In Algonquin

Interpretive Day Trails for All Levels

The Highway 60 Corridor that cuts through the southern section of Algonquin Provincial Park boasts 14 different interpretive day use hiking trails. Each trail showcases unique landscapes, ecology, wildlife, and history. These unique features are illustrated in companion trail guides which are read in conjunction with interpretive trail markers on each trail.
